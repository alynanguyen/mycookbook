    Banana Bread

    Information


    Time: 1.5 hours
    Difficulty: 1/5
    Tools: whisker(optional), kitchen scale, measuring cups (optional)
    Calories: 1062 (the whole loaf)

    Ingredients


    2-3 bananas (depending on the size), peeled
    38g unsalted butter, melted
    1/4 tsp baking soda
    1/2 pinch salt
    35g sugar (less or more of choice)
    1/2 large egg
    1/2 tsp vanilla extract
    80g all-purposed flour

    Papparoti

    Information


    Time: 2 - 5 hours
    Difficulty: 3/5
    Tools: kitchen scale, kneading mat (optional), pipping bag, baking mat (recommended)
    Calories: 303 calories/bun (6 buns total)

    Ingredients


    Bread dough
    180g flour (all-purposed/bread)
    25g caster sugar
    2g salt
    4g instant yeast
    1/2 egg (approx. 25g)
    120ml milk (1% fat)
    30g unsalted butter
    Fillings
    Of choice, eg. chocolate, custard, cream cheese, etc.


    Coffee topping
    2g black coffee
    5ml milk/water
    1/2 egg (approx.25g)
    35g icing sugar
    40g flour
    40g unsalted butter

    Tuiles

    Information


    Time: 30 minutes
    Difficulty: 1/5
    Tools: whisker, kitchen scale, measuring cups (optional)
    Calories: 500 (12-20 pieces)

    Ingredients


    40g sliced almond
    1 egg white
    20g caster sugar
    1.5ml vanilla extract
    1 tiny pinch salt
    10g all-purposed flour
    1/2 lemon zest (optional)
    15g unsalted butter, melted

    Tiramisu

    Information


    Time: 30 minutes
    Difficulty: 1/5
    Tools: whisker, kitchen scale, measuring cups (optional)
    Calories: 1639 (the whole bowl)

    Ingredients


    1.5-2 pasteurized eggs (approx. 75g), separated
    250g mascarpone, room temp.
    1/4 tsp salt
    50g sugar
    100g ladyfinger
    1 cup strong coffee
    1 tbsp cocoa powder
